About Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, officially the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, is an island country in South Asia. Known as the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," it offers diverse landscapes from golden beaches to misty mountains, ancient cities to modern metropolises.

The country is renowned for its rich biodiversity, UNESCO World Heritage Sites, tea plantations, and warm hospitality. Whether you seek adventure, relaxation, culture, or wildlife, Sri Lanka has something for every traveler.
